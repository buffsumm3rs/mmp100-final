Appetizer: Bacon Wrapped Dates Stuffed with Manchego

Ingredients:

  • 15 Medjool pitted dates
  • 1 pound aged Manchego, cut into 15 blocks
  • 5 slices bacon, cut into thirds
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Make an incision on one side of each date and insert a block of Manchego. Squeeze the date shut in your hand to secure it.
  3. Wrap each date with a strip of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
  4. Place all the wrapped dates on a sheet tray lined with a silicone mat or parchment paper.
  5. Roast the dates in the oven until the bacon on top is starting to crisp, about 5 to 6 minutes.
  6. Turn the dates over and cook the other side until the bacon crisps.
  7. Remove the dates from the oven and brush both sides with maple syrup.
  8. Return the dates to the oven and roast until they are really crackly and sticky, about 1 to 2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
